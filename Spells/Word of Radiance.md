# Word of Radiance
sources: [[Xanathar's Guide to Everything]] pg 171
tags: [[Cleric]], [[cantrip]], [[Evocation Spells]]

**Evocation cantrip**

*Casting time*: 1 action

*Range*: 5 feet

*Components*: V, M (a holy symbol)

*Duration*: Instantaneous
